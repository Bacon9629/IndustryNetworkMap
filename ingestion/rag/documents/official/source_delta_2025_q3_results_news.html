Delta Electronics, Inc. Announces 2025-Q3 Financial Results

TAIPEI, October 29, 2025 – Delta Electronics, Inc. today held a meeting of the Board of Directors, at which the Board approved the Company’s financial results for the third quarter of 2025. The consolidated sales revenues were NT$150,318 million, net income after tax(1) was NT$18,606 million, and earnings per share were NT$7.16.
       
Note (1): Profit during the period attributable to owners of parent company.
